# Contributing to Looked

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Looked! Whether you're fixing a bug, adding a new feature, or improving documentation, your help is appreciated.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Fork the Repository**: Create your own copy of the project.
2.  **Create a Branch**: `git checkout -b feature/your-feature-name`.
3.  **Make Changes**: Implement your changes and ensure they follow the project's style.
4.  **Test Your Changes**: Verify that the app still runs as expected.
5.  **Submit a Pull Request**: Describe your changes and why they are needed.

## Code of Conduct

Please be respectful and helpful to others in the community.

## Feedback

If you find a bug or have a feature request, please open an issue on GitHub.
